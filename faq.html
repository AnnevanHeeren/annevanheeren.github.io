Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

A: You can use your HZ Student Card to print, but first you need to charge it with credit.


Q: How can you scan a document a send it to your laptop at HZ?

A: The printers at school have a scan option, use your HZ Student Card for that, and your scan will automatically be sent to you by email.


Q: What do you need to do when you are sick / show symptoms of coronavirus?

A: Stay home! You can follow online lessons, but only if you really have to.


Q: How can you book a project space in one of the wings?

A: Go to Learn > click on the Selfservice portal widget > select 'new reservation' > Book project group rooms > you can now select time & location.


Q: What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

A: You can use your HZ Student Card to enter the parking lot.
